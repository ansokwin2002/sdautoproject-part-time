# **App Name**: SD AutoCar Showcase

## Core Features:

- Homepage Showcase: Visually appealing display of featured cars with brief descriptions and quick links to detailed information.
- Service Listings: Comprehensive list of services offered, such as maintenance, repair, detailing, etc., each with a description and pricing information.
- Photo Gallery: Showcase high-quality images of SD AutoCar's work, highlighting various car models, completed services, and before/after transformations.
- Team Bios: Introduce the team members with photos and short professional backgrounds, emphasizing expertise and customer care values.
- Contact Form & Information: A user-friendly contact form, plus displayed contact details such as phone number, email, and physical address, integrated with a map, allowing users to reach out for inquiries or service requests.
- Dynamic FAQs: An AI tool that generates relevant responses to commonly asked questions about car services, leveraging data from existing content to ensure accuracy and helpfulness.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Dark Blue (#1A237E) to convey trust and reliability.
- Background color: Light Gray (#F5F5F5) for a clean, modern feel.
- Accent color: Teal (#008080) for highlights and interactive elements.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a sans-serif font, will be used throughout for a modern, clean, and readable appearance. 
- Use clean, modern icons, from a set like 'Font Awesome', related to cars and services.
- Employ a responsive, grid-based layout ensuring the website is easily navigable and visually appealing on all devices.
- Incorporate subtle animations like fading transitions and smooth scrolling to enhance user experience without being distracting.
